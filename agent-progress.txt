PROJECT OVERVIEW
----------------
Goal: A native SwiftUI app for iOS 26 + macOS 26 that reimagines ByteDance's
豆包爱学 K12 AI-learning app — same surface area but better integrated, more
intuitive, optimized, and enhanced with Apple-platform intelligence.

root group (drop .swift files into 豆包爱学/ and they compile automatically).
Module name: DoubaoAiXue. Platforms: iphoneos, iphonesimulator, macosx.
